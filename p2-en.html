Design Thinking Project — Human-Centered Innovation Training

These projects were initiated in response to collaboration needs from corporate, nonprofit, and educational sectors. The goal was to help participants with non-design backgrounds grasp the principles of design thinking and develop innovative solutions through structured workshops.

My Role

  • Served as facilitator and instructional designer
  • Customized content based on participants’ backgrounds
  • Led user interviews and insight synthesis
  • Designed workshop flow and guided ideation and prototyping
  • Provided real-time feedback and post-workshop reflections

Methods & Process

01 Empathize

Understand user needs and pain points through research

02 Define

Synthesize observations and define the core problem

03 Ideate

Generate diverse ideas and potential solutions

04 Prototype & Test

Create quick prototypes and gather user feedback

Reflection

This project deepened my understanding that design thinking is not just a set of tools, but a mindset and collaborative language. It became a powerful bridge for cross-functional teams to align on goals and co-create effective solutions.
