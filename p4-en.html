Online Consumer Behavior Project｜Brand Strategy and Content Execution

Course Project | Oct 2023 – Dec 2023
Client: YiDiJing (Dish Soap Brand)

Project Goal

Targeting consumers under 35, we aimed to enhance brand visibility and engagement through social content strategies and communication refinement.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Analyzed online consumer behavior to identify trends and segment the target audience.
  • Planned and executed a Dcard campaign including copywriting, posting, and engagement tactics.
  • One post reached the front page and achieved nearly 98,000 views and 999 likes.
  • Worked closely with the brand to realign messaging and reposition the product for young consumers.

Strategies & Tactics

01｜Dcard Campaign

Two creative posts placed organically in popular forums with planned comment interactions.

02｜Xiaohongshu KOL Seeding

Collaborated with 5 lifestyle KOLs for video reviews covering cooking, family, and everyday scenarios.

03｜Reels Public Challenge

Launched a social challenge to increase visibility and promote a positive brand image.

Results

  • Dcard: 98,000+ views, viral reach without paid ads.
  • Xiaohongshu: 5 KOLs across segments with organic content cost ranging NT$0–500.
  • Reels challenge reached 30,000+ impressions with strong university student engagement.

Reflection

Low-involvement products like dish soap can break through by leveraging relatable storytelling and native content. Future opportunities include long-term ambassador programs and student-centered campaigns to deepen retention.
